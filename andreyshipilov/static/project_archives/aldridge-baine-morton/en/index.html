About us

Aldridge Baine Morton specializes in the field of direct search and personnel selection at the management and executive level. We offer the full range of executive search and professional recruitment services. We work with multinational and Russian companies operating in different fields of business such as FMCG, Oil & Gas, Automotive, IT/Telecom etc. We work throughout Russia and CIS.

All our consultants have extensive work experience with the FORTUNE 500 companies. The teams are formed in accordance with the matrix principle. Every client has a consultant who supervises the work on all positions. By identifying clients’ needs correctly we complete the recruitment process in short terms.

Aldridge Baine Morton is the only company on the Russian market that is providing full range of implant solution services. Accordingly to this innovative technology the project team works on the client’s territory. The team is responsible for all the recruitment process and closers all vacancies of the company. This technology helps you save time and recruitment budget.
