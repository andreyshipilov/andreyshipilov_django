Sport Express

Killy&Co are fully satisfied with the progress of works

Yesterday the visit of the IOC Coordination Commission on preparation of ХХII Winter Olympic Games in Sochi was completed. It was a second official visit to the capital of the White Olympics by a group of IOC experts and international winter sports federations. Such visits have been taking place once a year so far, and as the Games approach, IOC specialists will be visiting twice as frequently. But this only applies to official trips. Actually, Jean-Claude Killy, a triple Olympic champion, member of IOC heading the Coordinating Commission, and many of his colleagues visit Sochi much more often. After his accession to office a year and a half ago, the Frenchman has been a frequent guest of Russia’s resort capital. This is his sixth visit. His seventh visit is also in the pipeline — according to Killy, he and his colleagues will visit Sochi in six weeks’ time.

“Preparation for the Games is no small feat,” said the Chairman of the Coordination Commission. “We fully trust your specialists — they are all professionals of the highest grade, but the control over the preparation is essential”.

IOC representatives and Russian leaders of all levels, including two Vice Prime Ministers — Alexander Zhukov and Dmitry Kozak, spent two days in conference. They discussed the reports of IOC experts and representatives of international federations who had visited the facilities under construction just before the visit of the core group of the Coordination Commission members.

Journalists, including SE’s reporter, followed their footsteps, only a day later. And just as the foreign guests they were surprised at how fast things are changing here in Sochi. This time the hosts gave us a ride on a brand-new cable-way as far as plateau Rosa Khutor, then we went down by car to the finishing zone for all ski disciplines within the close vicinity of the future mountain Olympic village, we also visited the site of the future construction of the snowboard park and freestyle center, which will also be located near plateau Rosa Khutor.

The visit of the Coordination Commission was completed with a traditional press conference, where a high assessment was given to the work done by Sochi residents. IOC representatives remarked that “the mentality of the Russian organizers of the White Olympics has dramatically changed environment-wise” and that the "Games will become the most compact ones in the entire history — within only 35 minutes one will be able to get from the Olympic Park to Krasnaya Polyana". Vice Prime Ministers, in their turn, provided specific figures on the construction. Kozak stated the cost of the Games subject to the current year prices — it is 206 billion roubles, of those only 118 will be earmarked from the budget, while the rest will be provided by private investors. Meanwhile, Zhukov spoke about the Organizational Committee’s budget — 1.8 billion dollars, of which only 330 billion would be earmarked from the budget, while the rest will be provided by IOC and its sponsors.

By Lev ROSSOSHIK

Jean-Claude KILLY, Chairman of the Coordination Commission of IOC:
“We have been very pleased with the amount of energy and work that the Sochi 2014 organisers have committed to the Games. However, as I’ve said since our first meeting here, the entire Sochi team needs to keep up this pace in order to prepare the best Games in history.”
