The “Rosa Khutor” Project Presented in the Fourth Economic Forum Kuban-2005

The Ministry of Economic Development and Trade of the Russian Federation carried out the forum together with the Krasnodar region’s administration and personnel of the Presidential Plenipotentiary Envoy to the South Federal District with the assistance of the Government of the Russian Federation. The main issues were the development of tourist and transport complex in Russia and investments in innovations. Sochi-2014 round table focused on construction of the alpine ski resort Rosa Khutor which was related to the concept of the Winder Olympics.

“It was extremely important for us to know the position and plans of the region's administration and local authorities on infrastructure development in the region to make correct plans for our investments in construction of the resort”, commented participant of the forum Nicola Popov, director general of Rosa Khutor company.
