FIS confirmed: "Rosa Khutor" is ready for the Games

FIS representatives expressed full assurance that preparations for the Olympic Games in Sochi are going according to the schedule. They also confirmed that ski resort “Rosa Khutor” is ready for competitions.

During the third inspection visit to Sochi world-wide recognized FIS experts in winter sports made sure that the preparation for the Olympic Games is progressing successfully. FIS Delegation visited the ski center “Rosa Khutor” – Olympic facility for mountain ski competitions at Olympic Games of 2014, where in February of 2011 three years prior to the winter Games test Euro Cup mountain ski competitions will be held.

Upon results of the visit vice president of FIS Alpine Committee Bernhard Russi said: “Competitions on the pistes of “Rosa Khutor” may be held even tomorrow if such is required. We are pleased with all what we have seen. Upon completion of works concerning snow generating system and installation of a protection fencing it will be possible to provide training and competitions in mountain skiing”.
The main race director of World Cup among women Attle Skaardhal noticed: “A lot is done. We are fully assured that the remained works will be completed in time and “Rosa Khutor” will welcome world level competitions very soon. In autumn we are planning to return to the facility to make sure that the ski center is completely ready for Euro Cup competitions in February 2011”.

Federation experts confirmed that works are going on schedule and resort will be ready three years before the Olympic Games in Sochi, after descending from the pistes and installing of gates on the downhills pistes for men and women. FIS delegation has noticed that after Olympic Games this center will become one of the greatest world winter sports resorts with a series of world scale pistes.

The president of the Organizational Committee 'Sochi 2014' Dmitry Chernyshenko declared: “Preparation for Olympic Games is going on the highest level and according to the schedule. Organizational Committee introduces best world practices and experience to make the Games the most innovative and inspiring in history. That is why positive estimation of FIS experts is very important for us”.

FIS delegation consisted of vice president of Alpine Committee Bernhard Russi, main woman mountain ski World Cup Race Director Attle Skaardhal, man mountain ski World Cup Race Director Helmut Schmaltz, woman mountain ski Race Director Jan Tischhauser, mountain ski World Cup Coordinator Markus Walder. The inspection was provided jointly by woman World Cup Race Director in technical disciplines Markus Mayer, Person in charge of equipage and finishing zone of woman competitions Andreas Conner, woman mountain skiing Euro Cup Coordinator Janez Flerae.

The ski resort “Rosa Khutor” is located on Aibga crest and is one of the prioritized Olympic construction facilities. The unique peculiarity of the resort is the single finishing zone for all ski disciplines as well as the Olympic piste with 1000 meters altitude difference and six flying lengths up to 70 meters. The pistes were designed by a world known expert, vice president of FIS Alpine Committee Bernhard Russi. Total length of Olympic pistes is 9 kilometers, the complex will have 18 000 seats for spectators as per information of Press Service “Sochi -2014”.
