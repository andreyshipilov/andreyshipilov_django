May 06, 2010

June 07, 2010 →

Company for Development of Ski Resort Rosa Khutor and The Rezidor Hotel Group have entered into Agreement on Management of two hotels on the territory of Ski Resort Rosa Khutor.

Hotels Radisson Resort Rosa Khutor for 180 rooms and Park Inn Resort Rosa Khutor for 200 are to be located on the territory of Ski Resort Rosa Khutor (Krasnaya Polyana, Sochi). After completion of the resort construction we will see the opening of alpine skiing tracks of total length of about 80 km, 18 elevators, numerous restaurants, shops and an alpine skiing school. The Complex will host up to 10,500 people a day. During the Olympiad-2014, Ski Resort Rosa Khutor will host competitions in alpine skiing, snowboarding and freestyle, with 22 set of medals.

Opening of hotels of The Rezidor Hotel Group is scheduled for the fourth quarter of 2011. Radisson Resort Rosa Khutor, besides its 180 rooms, will offer to its guests a number of restaurants, spa and fitness center, and a conference hall. Guests of Park Inn Rosa Khutor for 200 rooms built 200 meters away from Radisson will be able to enjoy a restaurant on the hotel territory, as well as recreational and conference services of the neighboring hotel.
