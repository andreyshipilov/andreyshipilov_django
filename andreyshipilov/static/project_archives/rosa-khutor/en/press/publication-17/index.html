Dmitry Medvedev inspected sports facilities which are being built in the ski resort “Rosa Khutor”

Photo by presidential press service

Ski resort located in the settlement Esto-Sadok will welcome events of 11 competitive sports.

The cable way of the ski resort starts at the altitude of 550 meters above sea level, while Olympic village will be located at the altitude of 1100 meters. Sergei Bachin, director of the resort, says that according to the international standards the lower start point of the pistes will be located at the altitude of 960 meters and the highest one– at 2400 meters. The cable way will start operating in test mode from December 15, and in February 2011 the resort will welcome Russian mountain ski Championship and Euro Cup.

The head of “Olympstroy” company Taimuraz Bolloev reported to the President that all mountain Olympic facilities in Sochi are built in time or even ahead of schedule. Sledding and bobsleigh track should be built within 36 months, but it will be completed in 22 months. According to Mr. Bolloev, mountain cluster has 52 objects, 5 of them are for competitions: complex of ski jumps, sledding and bobsleigh track, stadium for snowboard, freestyle, biathlon complex and downhill skiing stadium. Considering the experience of Olympic Games in Vancouver where the competitions were held with positive ambient temperatures we plan to use in Sochi a system of artificial snow generation.

The program of test activities and contractual obligations bind Russian party to provide international competitions of the highest level in all the competitive sports, — reported the president of the Organizational Committee “Sochi-2014” to the head of state Dmitry Chernyshenko . In the next three sports seasons Sochi will welcome 5 thousands sportsmen and around 250 thousands sports lovers. Dmitry Medvedev was shown a video presentation of sports facilities where test competitions will be held.
