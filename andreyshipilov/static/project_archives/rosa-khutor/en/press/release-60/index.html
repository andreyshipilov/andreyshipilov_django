Rosa Khutor LLC and Compagnie des Alpes (France) reached a strategic partnership agreement on management of the Rosa Khutor Ski Resort

Rosa Khutor LLC and Compagnie des Alpes (France) reached a strategic partnership agreement on management of the Rosa Khutor Ski Resort (Sochi, Krasnaya Polyana). For the Rosa Khutor Ski Resort with the Stage 1 to open in the 2010-2011 season, the partnership with Compagnie Des Alpes, one of the world’s leaders in resort management, is an important stage in development of its own resort’s brand of the international standard.

Since its foundation in 1989, Compagnie des Alpes has become the acknowledged leader in leisure industry in Europe. The company owns and operates 15 popular ski resorts in the Alps (including the most famous Tignes, Val d’Isère, Les Arcs, La Plagne, Les Menuires, Les 2Alpes, Méribel, Chamonix) and 21 leisure parks (including Parc Astérix, Grévin and Walibi) in 6 European countries: France, Switzerland, the Netherlands, Belgium, Germany, and the United Kingdom. In 2010, the total number of visitors to the Compagnie des Alpes ski resorts amounts to more than 23 million people.

Compagnie des Alpes is controlled by its main shareholder , Caisse des Depots, the French State Bank.

The parties approved the appointment of Jean-Marc Farini, the former manager of one of the largest Compagnie des Alpes ski resorts in France, to the position of the General Manager of the ski resort. Also, the group of managers from Compagnie des Alpes will provide support in operating the Rosa Khutor resort. The first test events at the Rosa Khutor – the Russia Cup and one of the European Cup series in Alpine skiing in February 2011 – will be held under the direct supervision of the French specialists.

Cooperation of Compagnie des Alpes and the Rosa Khutor Ski Resort will provide for the ideal balance of sport, commercial, and recreational infrastructure of the resort, promoting the development of popular alpine skiing in Russia, creation of the facilities for the elite sport and training of the Olympic reserve, advancing the Russian tourism competitiveness and quality of services, and attracting tourists to the region due to the year-round operation of the resort.

“Partnership with Compagnie des Alpes provides the opportunity to implement the company’s unique experience in ski resort management for development of the Rosa Khutor Ski Resort,” - said Sergey Bachin, Director General of Rosa Khutor. “We believe that in cooperation with the Compagnie des Alpes specialists, we’ll be able to create the best ski resort in Russia which can compete with the best world resorts”.

“Rosa Khutor is a fantastic project and we are proud to be able to fully contribute to its success by bringing our world leader’s expertise “, said Serge Naïm, President of CDA Management.

Ski Resort Development Company Rosa Khutor LLC was established in 2003 by Interros Holding Company with the purpose to build and to develop the Rosa Khutor Ski Resort in the neighbourhood of Krasnaya Polyana village, not far from the city of Sochi.
The project involves the creation of a unique year-round world class ski resort with some Olympic facilities, including Ski Center, Snowboard Park, Freestyle Center and Mountain Olympic Sub-village.
