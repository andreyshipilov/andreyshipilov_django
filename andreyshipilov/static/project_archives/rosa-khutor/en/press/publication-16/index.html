Vedomosti, 103 (2621)

Visiting «Interros»

By Elena Vinogradova and Anton Filatov

Hotels built at the ski resort “Rosa Khutor” will be managed by companies Rezidor, Accor, Swissotel, Tulip and Heliopark. «Interros» managed to transfer the ownership of eight from 10 hotels which are being constructed.

“Rosa Khutor” company (incorporated into “Interros” holding belonging to Vladimir Potanin) signed a contract for management of its eight hotels and two more are now negotiated, informed “Vedomosti” General Director of “Rosa Khutor” Sergei Bachin. The hotel complex totally has 1636 rooms. Six hotels located in the lower part of the settlement will be put into exploitation in 2011 – 2012, and in 2013 the remaining four will be opened in the upper part. Contracts are signed with Rezidor Hotel Group operators (two hotels — Park Inn and Radisson SAS), Golden Tulip Hospitality Group (two hotels— Golden Tulip and Tulip Inn), Accor (two hotels with Mercure brand). Swissotel and Russian hotel network Heliopark will operate one hotel each. President of Heliopark Alexander Gusakov confirmed this Information. Rezidor company declared its intention for cooperation with “Rosa Khutor” already on the 5-th of May. Participation of Golden Tulip Hospitality Group was confirmed by its employee, there is no contract yet with Accor but there is a verbal agreement, as per source close to the management of the company. Unfortunately we could not contact with the representatives of Swissotel yesterday. Large number of brands is an advantage for the project as each operator has its own category of consumers, says Senior vice-president of Knight Frank Andrew Zakrevsky.

Total investments amount in “Rosa Khutor” project – including construction of the ski center, freestyle center, snowboard park – is estimated as 1,4-1,5 billion USD. Half of this amount will be invested in hotels.

Organizational Olympic Committee “Sochi -2014” informed that all together the city should have 42 437 new hotel rooms, 2941 of them are five stars rooms, 10 630 — four stars room, 23 060 — three stars rooms and 5806 – two stars rooms. As per information of CB Richard Ellis, recent city hotel vacancy is around 5000 capacity. Earlier, Deputy Director for Development of CB Richard Ellis Stanislav Ivashkevich said that it is not possible to build such a great number of hotels (above 200): there is simply nobody to operate them plus a strong deficiency of qualified personnel.

In the beginning of June “Yug – Novi vek” company (incorporated into “Snegiri development”) informed about signing an agreement with Hyatt International, that will operate a hotel with 200 numbers and sell 70 apartments. The amount of investments in this project is 225 million USD. Another 4000 hotel rooms (majorly 3-4 stars ones) will be built by ACT Group according to announcement of the owner Telman Ismailov. Hotels in Sochi are also built by GAZPROM, JSC “Krasnaya Polyana” and President Administration.
