FIS representatives inspected Olympic venues of Rosa Khutor Alpine ski resort – snowboard-park and freestyle center

Delegation of the International Ski Federation (FIS), headed by freestyle Coordinator Joe Fitzgerald, visited Alpine ski resort Rosa Khutor with regular inspection in July 21- 22, 2011. The purpose of inspectors consisted in estimation of progress in construction of future Olympic venues – freestyle center and snowboard-park, where first test events are to take place during the season 2011/2012. Members of the delegation inspected tracks under construction for ski – cross, snowboard – cross, mogul, aerial and half-pipe and noted significant progress in construction.

Besides location and parameters of general-purpose trail for slopestyle – new discipline of freestyle and snowboard, included from July 4, 2011 into winter Olympic games program-, were determined. Selected solution effectually combines the interests of sportsmen and sideliners, who will be able to see from viewing stands the most entertaining part of the track.

Foreign specialists noticed positive dynamics in snowboard-park and freestyle center construction and agreed, that the following inspection visit is to take place at the end of September, 2011.
