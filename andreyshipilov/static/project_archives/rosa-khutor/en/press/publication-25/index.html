Sport Express

Rosa Khutor is ahead of the schedule

I was here a year ago — in February 2008. Last year I visited Rosa Khutor again, which is one of the most important elements of the mountain cluster (or, if you please, zone, district), at whose facilities, which are being built presently, as many as 20 sets of Olympic awards will be fought for in 2014: half in mountain skiing, another half in snowboard and freestyle. Also, now Rosa Khutor Company has been entrusted with the construction of the mountain Olympic village for 2,600 guests (earlier it was planned to build it in a different place).

When a year ago we rose in a huge helicopter MI-8 to the peak of 2,320 meters, only obscure wooden structures could be seen over the virgin snow. We were explained that a few years later it would be the point of start for the Olympic downhill skiing — one of the most spectacular mountain skiing disciplines. Back then we decided to rise here every year, in order to note down the changes that would be taking place.

This time the helicopter was a smaller one, КА-32, and it was unable to land on the same pad on the peak — it was only able to remain hanging at the height of two meters. In order to avoid getting into the air current, we had to jump right into the snow drifts. However, after we had emerged from the snow, we saw the changes that had occurred within the year: the concrete structure of the future starting point, instead of the wooden timbering, meteorological installations, which allowed with the help of the Internet to monitor weather changes at any place in the world, avalanche protection structures, green roofs Gazprom complex standing out against the general background of whitish-grey (this year there has not been much snow), from where the day before we set out to Psekhako plateau by cable road, where skiing races will take place in five years’ time.

At the same time we saw the first piles of the would-be cargo port, which by the 1st July of 2010 (mind the date!) will have been commissioned on the border of Imeretin Valley, and the first kilometres of the railroad, in other words — the first concrete sites of the freight terminal for the essential inert (crushed stone and sand) cargo in the coastal village Mirny. We also saw the teeming activity of the builders at the sections of the combined road Adler - "Alyshka-Service", where not only outlines of temporary bridges are visible, but also the main structures with six railway and three motor vehicle tunnels.

I must admit that I had been ready to see all that — I just saw with my own eyes what I had seen in the photographs at the office of the President of the organizational Committee Sochi-2014 Dmitry Chernyshenko, whom I have interviewed recently.
But when I found myself at the lower base of mountain resort "Rosa Khutor", I was simply dumbstruck by what I saw. Please believe me: neither a year ago, nor half a year ago there had been anything of the kind! Now, together with the guide, Director General of the company of the same name Sergey Trubin, I toured the “insides” of two most beautiful buildings styled as European chalets — the service building and cable car garage, which constitute the so-called lower base of the complex of "Rosa Khutor". Already today they are ready to receive future athletes, spectators and just amateur skiers willing to test the Olympic courses. According to Trubin, the main tasks for the forthcoming year would be homologation of the courses (in other words, certification of the same at the International Skiing Federation), complete installation of ski lifts and approbation of the artificial snow system.

We had no doubts that everything will be completed on time and according to the highest quality standards even later, when, like a year before, we were taken by the ratrac to the height of 1,500 metres. In place of virgin snow plateau there now was a concrete structure of the “Mountain haven of Rosa Khutor" — the future local catering center, the construction of which had begun recently. They promised to treat us to a dinner in one of its restaurants within a year.

I already look forward to this future dinner at the Olympic “haven”. Two years ago I believed that Sochi would win the tough competition for the right to organize Games-2014. A year ago, when I heard from Trubin the date of the first official start of the World Cup of the local piste - 12 February, 2012, I announced it in the SE. Now I am convinced that the International Federation has postponed the date of the future test start too late - "Rosa Khutor" will be ready to receive the participants of the "white circus" much earlier.

By Rossoshik L
