4 lifts are open for the season 2010/2011 in Rosa Khutor: Olympia, National Forest, Caucasus Express and Wolf Rock. Total length of open runs (или slopes/ pistes) is 15 km, including the Olympic ones — 9 km.

When the resort construction is completed, it will have 18 lifts, pistes with a total length of about 80 km. The pistes will be located between 575 and 2,320 m above sea level, the average height is 1,745 m. The maximum resort capacity will be 10,500 skiers per day, piste capacity will be 9,500 skiers per day.

Kid's Club Top

The kids’ club is a place where children can spend several hours learning and playing. Your child will learn how to ski with professional instructors on a slope especially reserved for kids, and equipped with a beginners’ Magic Carpet conveyor lift. Your kid will also get to spend time with peers on our playground, safely supervised by qualified daycare teachers.
