Commercial real estate of ski resort “Rosa Khutor” is equally convenient both for comfortable rest and living, and for successful business activities. Hotels and suites, restaurants and cafes, stores and stalls — absolutely all the resort facilities represent a symbiosis of effective solutions in the field of design, construction and service, as well as ergonomics and comfort.

Buying or renting real estate at “Rosa Khutor” resort you not only get a unique asset, which can meet the highest demands, but also get an excellent opportunity to find yourself in the “heart” of the forthcoming Olympics 2014 — one of the main events in the life of modern Russia.

Hotels Top

Ski resort “Rosa Khutor” united a line of popular brands of worldwide famous hotels, represented in the categories from 3 to 5 stars. 10 hotels with total hotel room capacity of 1660 rooms will be constructed on the territory of the resort, each of them will be managed by professional hotel operators, such as Rezidor, Accor, Golden Tulip and others.

Each separate hotel was designed with the immediate participation of the operator, which allowed to include the most up-to-date technical, architectural and design solutions into the hotel concepts.

No matter what resort hotel our guest chooses, he must be 100% sure of high quality and comfort of his rest.

Hotels of ★★★ category will be represented by such brands as Park Inn, Tulip Inn, Mercure, Heliopark and Extreme. These hotels will be ideal for people, who can appreciate comfort at a reasonable price. Moreover, three-star hotels offers would seem very attractive for representatives of business communities, interested in conducting their corporate events in such a unique place as Krasnaya Polyana.

Hotels of ★★★★ category will be represented by such brands as Golden Tulip and Radisson. Designed with due account of the highest standards, these hotels will be to the taste of all who need high service and comfort level.

Salespots Top

Resort “Rosa Khutor” offers excellent opportunities for business. Extensive trade and entertainment infrastructure will be built within the framework of the project, including: premises for stores of different formats, trade and entertainment center, skating-rink, SPA-center, night-club, restaurants and cafes.

All premises are designed with an account of the modern requirements made to the trade real estate. Effective planning solutions, wide shop windows, superb visibility, separate entrance, opportunity for convenient signboards distribution — all these are the characteristics of the resort's trade areas. All premises, including those designed for cafes and restaurants, are equipped with all required communications and fully ready for fine finishing.

Key parameters of trade real estate of the resort

• Rented area of premises —
over 40 000 square meters.
• Anchor tenants: supermarket, sports goods, entertainment center.
• Trade and entertainment center with a gallery.
• 180 stores.
• 15 restaurants and cafes.

The resort streets will allow to place over 100 stores of the street retail format with the area of 30 to 200 square meters, with an opportunity of block combination. All stores will have excellent visibility, big show windows and a separate entrance.

A trade and entertainment center will be built within the framework of the project, with a total area of over 10,000 square meters. The center will include a supermarket, trade gallery, bowling, billiard and other entertainments.

It is planned to place a great number of cafes and restaurants on the territory of the resort. The corresponding premises have the area of 100 to 1,500 square meters and are equipped with all required utilities.

Premises for rent

  • For a store or a stall in the shopping street
  • For a store or a stall in the trade and entertainment center
  • For restaurants and cafes
  • Business terms

The ground floors of the public buildings of the resort allow to place stores of different formats with the area of 30 to 200 square miles, with an opportunity of their combination. It is expected that these areas will be mainly occupied by famous brands stores, points of services (Beauty saloons, photo studio, etc.), as well as hire stations of ski accessories and equipment.

The area of trade center allows accommodating stores of goods and services in various formats and categories with areas ranging from 30 to 300 square meters.

Depending on the requirements of the tenant it is possible to arrange a café or a restaurant having specific characteristics and effective layout planning on the territory of the ski resort.

Lease terms are available upon request.

Apartments Top

“Rosa Khutor” project includes more than 140,000 square meters of first-grade suites, intended for private residence. Everyone, who used to having rest with home coziness and comfort, is offered to consider purchase of suites in apartment houses, or isolated chalets, located at the height of more than 1,000 meters over the sea level.

Suites and Chalets “Rosa Khutor” — is modern architecture, effective planning, advanced constructions technologies and high quality engineering utilities. It is operational integrity and developed infrastructure for comfortable living and rest: stores, restaurants, sports and entertainment centers, SPA.

Suites, situated at the Low Base of the resort will suit ideally for people, preferring active rest and dynamic pastime. There will be dozens of stores, restaurants, cafes and a night club in immediate surround of suite buildings.

Planning of suites includes studios with the area of 34 square meters and over, as well as two-, three-, and four bedroom suites with the area up to 115 square meters.

Apartments, situated on the Plateau territory offer their hosts a chance to feel free from the city fuss and problems and have a good rest. Located at the height of more than 1,000 meters over the sea level surrounded by indescribable mountain beauty, this estate can give a myriad of unforgettable emotions from the superb year-round rest.

High-class Chalets, designed in the traditional Alpine style, are located on the territory of the Plateau and other adjacent territories. This estate is oriented towards the most demanding audience, who appreciates quiet, isolated rest.
