The Rosa Khutor Ski Resort Development Company was established in 2003 by the Interros company with the purpose of building and developing the alpine ski resort Rosa Khutor (in the neighborhood of the village Krasnaya Polyana not far from the city of Sochi). This project assumes the creation of a unique year-round environmental resort for Russia, harmoniously combining possibilities for alpine skiing and seaside relaxation. The construction of the Alpine Ski Resort Rosa Khutor is conducted according to advanced standards and requirements.

The construction of the mass world-class ski resort with pistes appropriate for learning skiing and for skiing at any skill level, as well as for the holding of sporting events, including the Olympic Games, World Championships and stages of World Cups in all the alpine competitive events.

The construction of the most up-to-datesnow-making system that will extend the ski-season from 140 to 180 days a year, depending on the location.

The creation of the required infrastructure for the comfortable reception and top-quality service at the resort for 10,500 guests daily.

The equipping of the resort with modern ski industry facilities, including hi-speed gondola lifts that get to the mountaintop in 20 min. (elevation 1745 m).

All-season operation of the resort, providing of a wide range of winter and summer sporting activities and entertainments.

The construction of the pistes with a total length of almost 80 km. Certification of the race courses for holding the international championships by International Ski Federation (FIS).

The use of modern environmental technologies will help to preserve the unique ecological system of the Rosa Khutor Plateau area.

— The Rosa Khutor Plateau — a unique place for the construction of an alpine ski resort, and according to the experts, a location equalled by none other in the world. Therefore INTERROS has undertaken this project with much enthusiasm. Future Olympic Games in Sochi will add to the importance and farsightedness of our initiative. I wait eagerly for that moment when it will be possible to ski on Rosa Khutor... I am sure that it will become not only the best Russian alpine ski resort, but will also rank as a serious competitor to any other in Europe.

Vladimir Potanin President of INTERROS Company

January 2001

Joint initiative of Olympic Committee of Russia and administrative authority of Krasnodar Territory on development of tourism and sports mountain climate complex “Krasnaya polyana” (TSMCC) in Sochi was encouraged by the Government of the Russian Federation.

2001—2002

According to the protocol of the meeting with the Chairman of the Government of the Russian Federation, dated June, 15 2001, technical evaluation of potential building sites of ski resort in the area of Krasnaya polyana, and also selection and detailed assessment of preferred sites were conducted by the international group of the largest world-class specialists, including the Companies “Ecosign Mountain Resort Planners Ltd.” and “Doppelmayr Seilbahnen GmbH”, on order of the Company “Interros”.

October 2002

Master plan on development of ski resort “Rosa Khutor” was prepared by the Company “Ecosign Mountain Resort Planners Ltd.” on order of the Company “Interros”.

February 2003

Order No. 238-р of the Government of the Russian Federation, determining the borders of Sochi national park sites to be lend-leased for building of priority objects within the framework of TSMCC, was accepted.

March 2003

Ski resort development company Rosa Khutor LLC was set up for realization of the project on building of ski resort on the plateau Rosa Khutor.

July 2003

Tripartite general cooperation agreement was signed between administrative authority of Krasnodar Territory, Olympic Committee of Russia and Ski resort development company Rosa Khutor LLC.

September 2003

The project was suspended due to undecided issues concerning the lease of land site and construction of infrastructure (roads, energy supply, water supply, gas supply etc.).

December 2004

Ski resort development company Rosa Khutor LLC won a competitive tender for the right to conclude the first of all contracts on long-term lease of land site on the plateau Rosa Khutor in area of 412.13 ha. Long-term land lease contract was concluded between Sochi national park and Ski resort development company Rosa Khutor LLC for the period of 49 years.

September 2005

Planning documentation was prepared by the general designer “ILPh Russland” for construction of the first stage of ski resort “Rosa Khutor”.

February 2006

Ski resort development company Rosa Khutor LLC became a winner of a competitive tender for the right to lease a land site in area of 125 ha in Sochi national park. Therefore, now Ski resort development company Rosa Khutor LLC leases out 537 ha in Sochi national park.

September 2006

The project on building of ski resort “Rosa Khutor” underwent ecological expertise successfully.
Positive conclusion of expert commission of state ecological expertise, confirmed by Rosprirodnadzor of the Ministry of Natural Resources of the Russian Federation, was obtained. Also, positive summary expert conclusion of extradepartmental expertise of Krasnodar Territory was issued.

October 2006

Building permission, issued by Administrative authority of Sochi, was obtained by Committee for architecture and urban development, for construction of the first stage of ski resort “Rosa Khutor”.

February 2007

The Company “Rosa Khutor” concluded a cooperation agreement with World Wildlife Fund for construction and putting into operation of a new ecological ski complex “Rosa Khutor”, eco-resort, on the territory of Sochi national park.

May 2007

The Company “Rosa Khutor” supported the project of World Wildlife Fund concerning the return of the West-Asian leopard to the Caucasus. The task of the project initiators is to return the West-Asian leopard to Russia.

February 2008

Project implementation area Top

The area of ski resort construction is located within 6 km to the East of Krasnaya Polyana village on the Western slopes of the Caucasus mountains within 50 km from Sochi.

The Rosa Khutor plateau covers over 1820ha of the Northern, the North-eastern and Southern slopes of the Aibga ridge, that mount from the Mzymta river up to the mountain range top on the Stone Post mountain.

It takes 40 min to get from the Adler International Airport to the resort site along the renovated federal expressway. After the construction of the combined motorway and railway road «Adler — mountain climatic resort Alpika-Service» it will take about 25 minutes by high-speed train.

The Rosa Khutor Plateau

The construction area of the alpine ski resort Rosa Khutor is about 550 ha on the Rosa Khutor plateau, and it is the most attractive from the point of view of alpine sports.

Pistes certified by FIS as suitable for holding of all Olympic alpine competitive events can be developed along the plateau slopes.

Characteristics of the mountain area

The slopes are oriented — northward and north-eastward.
The slopes’ gradient ranges from — 18% to 42%, with steeper slopes located higher than 1,5 km mark.
The slopes suitable for developing the race courses are about 1,466 km long.

Development stages Top

The development of the ski area is divided into three Stages. When the resort construction is completed, it will have 18 lifts, pistes with a total length of about 80 km.

The pistes will be located between 575 and 2320 m above sea level, the average height is 1745 m. The maximum resort capacity will be 10,500 skiers per day, piste capacity will be 9,500 skiers per day.

Stage 1 is the construction of eleven lifts, pistes with a length 38 km, including Pistes for tobogganing and bicycle tracks (for the summer season). The capacity of the pistes will be 5000 skiers per day.

Construction of lifts and pistes is planned during Stage 2 in the east sites near Urotchische Ober Khutor.

Stage 3 is the construction of lifts and skiing zones on the southern slope of Aibga ridge. The maximum capacity of the resort by the end of Stage 3 will be 10,500 skiers per day. Piste capacity will be 9,500 skiers per day.

Pistes

The resort's pistes are suitable for skiers of any skill level.

The most difficult «black» pistes for expert skiers are located between 1350m and 2320m.

«Red» pistes in the mid part of the eastern slopes of the Rosa Khutor Plateau are located between 940m and 1350m and are designed for intermediate and advanced skiers.

«Blue» pistes are located near Rosa Khutor Mountain Lodge and designed for children and beginners.

Infrastructure

Rosa Valley

The base area is located at an elevation of 560 m above sea level. Such infrastructure objects as hotels, aquapark, shops, pay-offices, café, ski school, changing rooms, rental stores and others are located here.

In addition, in the base area there are parking lots, repair shops, transformer stations etc.

Rosa Peak

The Rosa Peak is situated at an elevation of 2327 m above sea level near Lift C. A restaurant and a helicopter pad.

Rоsа Lounge

2/3-floored complex is the centre of the alpine ski resort located on the plateau at an elevation of 1166m above sea level. There is access to the pistes, training race courses, entertainment activities and service. Among the infrastructure installations there are 3 restaurants, a child care centre, shops, a first aid station, changing rooms, rental shops.

Rоsа Stadium

The Mountain Bar is located at an elevation of 940 m above sea level near Lift D. Within the area there are 2 administrative buildings, a hotel, 2 restaurants.
